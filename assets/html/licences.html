Licences

Welcome to Ekata SCS Licences...

Here compliance meets convenience in our Licensing Services. As a pivotal component of our comprehensive transportation solutions, Ekata specializes in obtaining and managing licenses essential for international trade, ensuring seamless and hassle-free logistics operations.

  • IEC Code: Ekata facilitates swift and efficient Importer-Exporter Code (IEC) registration, streamlining your entry into global trade with minimal bureaucratic hurdles.

  • Trade Licences: Navigate local and international regulations effortlessly with Ekata's Trade Licenses service. We ensure that your business adheres to all necessary licensing requirements, minimizing risks and ensuring compliance.

  • AD Code Registration: Ekata expedites the Authorised Dealer (AD) Code registration process, essential for international financial transactions. Our experts ensure a smooth and timely registration, allowing you to conduct cross-border business with ease.

  • CEPA: Unlock the advantages of preferential trade agreements with Ekata's Comprehensive Economic Partnership Agreement (CEPA) services. We facilitate the necessary documentation and compliance for seamless trade in CEPA member countries.

  • DGFT Licences: Ekata simplifies the Directorate General of Foreign Trade (DGFT) licensing process. Our experts manage the application and approval processes, ensuring your business meets the necessary regulatory standards for international trade.

  • Experience the Ekata SCS advantage with Licensing Services that prioritize your compliance needs. Trust us to handle the intricacies of obtaining and managing licenses, setting new benchmarks in licensing excellence for the transportation industry.
